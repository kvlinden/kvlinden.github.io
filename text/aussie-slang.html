Aussie slang is called (in slang) "True Blue Strine".

  • aggro: cross/upset; abbrev of "aggrivated"
  • alkie: alcoholic
  • appro: abbrev of "approval", something waiting for approval is "on appro"
  • arvo: afternoon
  • bikkie: biscuit
  • bizzo: business, eg. "all that bizzo"
  • bloke: man
  • brekkie: breakfast
  • carbie: carburettor
  • cardie: cardigan
  • chinwag: chat
  • chuck a wobbly: throw a fit
  • dag: someone being silly
  • dero: derelict
  • devo: deviant
  • doco: documentary
  • drongo: a dim-wit
  • duds: trousers
  • dunny: toilet
  • gander: look, eg. "just havin a gander"
  • garbo: bargage collecter
  • good nick: good condition
  • go bonkers: go crazy
  • jiffy: a short while
  • kip: nap
  • leftie: socialist
  • lippie: lipstick
  • mozzie: mosquito
  • muso: musician
  • nong: idiot
  • ocker: uncultured, chauvinistic, sports-mad Aussie bloke
  • on ya: well done
  • ooroo: goodbye
  • pash: passion
  • perler: great
  • pinko: communist symphathiser
  • possie: position
  • preggers: pregnant
  • rabbit on: talk incessantly
  • reccy: recreation
  • reffo: refugee
  • rego: registration
  • rellie: relative
  • roo: kangaroo
  • salvo: salvation army officer
  • sambo/sanger: sandwich
  • sarky: sarcastic
  • smoko: smoking break
  • suss: suspicious
  • telly: television
  • tinnie: tin of beer
